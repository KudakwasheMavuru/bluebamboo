About Us

Blue Bamboo Ltd is a company leading the environmental maximization and socio-economic development of bamboo through conservation, management, and industrialization of the bamboo ecosystem.

Mission

To lead conservation and industrialization of bamboo as a viable and environmentally attractive alternative fiber and fuel.

Vision

An Africa with zero deforestation and increased access to bamboo as an environmentally attractive alternative fiber

Values

Respect for nature
Team work
Creativity Transparency Sustainability

Products/Services
Bamboo Seedlings

We sell ready to plant high quality bamboo seedlings. Blue Bamboo is operating a state-of- the-art greenhouse an annual production capacity of more than 100,000 bamboo seedlings with different species.

Bamboo briquettes

High quality, clean and affordable bamboo briquettes produced from fast growing local bamboo species as a sustainable cooking fuel

Bamboo Packaging

Bamboo, when used as a material for packaging, offers a high level of durability along with versatility. Bamboo has a rapid growth rate, making it an abundant and biodegradable renewable resource.

Community Sustainable Projects

Toothbrushes Pulp,paper, paperboard Sanitary paper products bamboo fiber for textiles etc.

Impact

Our projects provide a myriad of long term biodiversity, climate change and social benefits, while providing an economically viable solution to achieve forest landscape restoration and the generation of healthy, functioning ecosystem.

No poverty

Elimination of poverty through training, job creation and employment opportunities to residents near Blue Bamboo’s business projects.

Good Health and Well-being

Blue Bamboo establishes good health and well-being through production of environmentally friendly products such as charcoal briquettes, that emit less C02 in the atmosphere in comparison to the fire wood, planting more bamboo species that provide clean breathing air to the community. Bamboos emit 35% more oxygen than other plants.

Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

Blue bamboo products manufacturing processes and technology promote innovation and foster industrialization.

Climate Action

Reduce the emission of CO2 in the atmosphere through production of bamboo charcoal briquettes, and use of biodegradable products such as cotton buds, toothbrushes, bamboo toilet papers, etc.

One of our goals is to influence more individuals to do the eco-friendly switch, and we do this by partnering with corporations and organizations that share the same mission.
